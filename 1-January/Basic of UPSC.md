UPSC - Union Public Service Commission

## 1.Indian Administrative Service (IAS) 
--------------------------------------
District administration, policy making
Roles: District Collector, Secretary, Chief Secretary

## 2.Indian Police Service (IPS)
--------------------------------
Law & order, internal security
Roles: SP, DIG, IG, DGP

## 3.Indian Forest Service (IFS – Forest)
------------------------------------
Environment, wildlife, climate policy
Roles: DFO, PCCF


### 1.Salaries 
## SDM / Assistant Collector    -->  0–4 yrs   ==>  ₹56,100
## District Magistrate (DM)     -->  4–9 yrs   ==>	₹67,700
## Director / Joint Secretary.  -->	 9–16 yrs  ==>	₹1,18,500
## Additional Secretary	        -->  16–25 yrs ==>	₹1,44,200
## Secretary (Govt of India)	-->  25+ yrs   ==> 	₹1,82,200
## Cabinet Secretary (Top)	    -->  30+ yrs.  ==>	₹2,50,000

## 2.ROLE OF A DISTRICT COLLECTOR
# A DM wears many hats at the same time:
# Head of district administration
# Revenue officer
# Law & order authority (District Magistrate)
# Head of disaster management
# Coordinator of all government departments

## 3.Office Hours ( 10 AM to 5 PM)
# Meetings with: SP (Police), Department heads, Local MLAs & MPs
# Reviewing: Government schemes, Development projects, Budget spending
# Public grievance hearings: Citizens directly meet the DM

## Powers of DM
# Can issue Section 144
# Controls district police (administratively)
# Controls land revenue & records
# Disaster authority (floods, riots, pandemics)
# Election authority in district
